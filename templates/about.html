About MindPredict

MindPredict is an AI-powered system designed to analyze text input and provide insights into possible mental health conditions such as Depression, Anxiety, Stress, Loneliness, Bipolar Disorder, Schizophrenia, or Normal state.

Our model uses Natural Language Processing (NLP) and Machine Learning to detect emotional patterns from user responses. This enables early awareness of mental well-being and helps individuals reflect on their mental state.

⚠️ Disclaimer: This tool is meant for educational and awareness purposes only. It is not a substitute for professional diagnosis or treatment. If you are experiencing mental health issues, please seek guidance from a certified mental health professional.
